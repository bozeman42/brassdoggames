micro miners

Micro Miners is two games in one! The first is a complete Deck Builder for 2 to 4 players that fits in your pocket. Delve deep into the strategy to optimise your deck of gems and carts. Pick your time to strike and load the final gem into your cart, or into your opponent's to capitalize on all of their effort.

Micro Miners can also be used to play a fast and strategic drafting game. Choose the gems you keep and which you pass along before manipulating the placement of the carts to your advantage.
